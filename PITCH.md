# 🎤 SevaLink AI: 1-Minute Pitch

“SevaLink AI is a real-time disaster response platform that connects people in need with nearby volunteers and NGOs instantly.

When a user reports a crisis, our system uses location-based intelligence and AI matching to assign the best helpers nearby.

With real-time maps, emergency broadcasting, and smart volunteer matching, SevaLink AI can significantly reduce response time during disasters and save lives.”
